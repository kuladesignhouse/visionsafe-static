EVAS + Embraer

EVAS has been widely adopted among operators of Private Jets. VisionSafe is thrilled to announce EVAS is now available on all Embraer business aircraft.

Smoke is the leading defined cause of emergency landing for ETOPS

After numerous incidences of smoke impeding pilots’ vision, and with catastrophic loss as a result, VisionSafe Corporation came up with the solution. Embraer has proven time and time again that it is an OEM committed to safety, and we are thrilled to announce EVAS is now available on all Embraer business aircraft, giving their clients the option of adding EVAS to their aircraft as an aftermarket upgrade.
